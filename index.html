Privacy Policy

App: iStory

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Introduction

iStory (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the iStory mobile application (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you use the Service.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or email, we collect your email address and name (if provided).
  • User ID: A unique identifier associated with your account.

Usage Data

  • Reading Progress: We store your reading progress, including which pages you have read and your choices in interactive stories.
  • App Usage: Information about how you interact with the app, including settings preferences and language preferences.

Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: We may collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, and device identifiers.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we collect and store your FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token to send you notifications.

Payment Information

  • In-App Purchases: When you make purchases through the app, payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card information. We only receive confirmation of successful transactions and credit allocations.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Deliver interactive story content and track your reading progress.
  • To manage your account: Create and manage your user account, process requests, and provide customer support.
  • To process payments: Process in-app purchases and manage your credits (if/when purchases are available).
  • To send notifications: Send push notifications about new content, updates, or important information (with your consent).
  • To improve our Service: Analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR – EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide and maintain the Service and your account.
  • Legitimate interests – to secure, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Consent – for push notifications and analytics (where applicable).
  • Legal obligations – to comply with applicable laws.

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

  • Your data may be stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud Platform).
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest (where supported by the provider and our configuration).
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect personal information.

Local Storage

  • Some data (such as reading progress) may be stored locally on your device for offline access.
  • This local data may be synchronized with our servers when you have an internet connection.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see “Account Deletion” below).

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by:

  1. Using the in-app “Delete Account” option (if available), or
  2. Submitting a request through our account deletion form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeFPP1b4jyCa5bi_80-L7_D_IXUUrmH-gah-loWXfnVSwIWmw/viewform

When we receive your request:

  • Your email address, user ID, authentication data, reading progress, and stored preferences will be permanently deleted within 14 days.
  • Push notification tokens will be removed.
  • Some data may be retained if required by legal obligations (for example, transaction records if you made in-app purchases).

After deletion, your account cannot be restored.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that support core app functionality:

Google Services

  • Firebase: Authentication, cloud storage, analytics, and push notifications.
  • Google Sign-In: User authentication.
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and (if enabled) in-app purchases.

Analytics and Tracking

  • Firebase Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app.
  • AppsFlyer: Attribution and analytics (only if enabled in the app build).

AI Services

  • Google Gemini API: We use Google’s Gemini AI to generate interactive story content based on your choices.

Your Rights and Choices

You may have the following rights regarding your personal information (depending on your location):

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate information be corrected.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (see “Account Deletion”).
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of push notifications through your device settings.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format (where applicable).
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw consent at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
